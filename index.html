Crystal Collector

Rules:

You are a collector in search of crystals to trade. While on this quest, you stumble upon a seemingly untouched cave covered in rare, colorful crystals. While obtaining the crystals, you hear a powerful voice call out, "You may only take what is allowed, or face the consequences!". You must return with the specified amount of crystals. No more, no less.

Match the amount of crystals below by clicking the on crystals themselves. Each crystal is worth a different number. It is up to you to figure out how much! Everytime you click a crystal, your crystal count adds up. If your current crystal count matches the number, you win. Good luck!
